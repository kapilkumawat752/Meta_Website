Experience Cloud- Experience Cloud Salesforce


Salesforce Experience Cloud facilitates your customers, partners, or employees with a common platform required for seamless communication. It incorporates services and features of Service Cloud, plus, provides additional benefits of creating a knowledge base, customer self-service portals, a portal for agent-customer interaction, and much more. Formerly it was known as Community Cloud, but, since it has been doing more than just creating communities online, it was rather renamed appropriately. Metadologie has experience in working with Community Cloud and now Experience Cloud, which can help your business, especially on the customer interaction front.

With the Experience Cloud, you can create various portals or say various platforms dedicated to each of your communication. Majorly, you can create the following portals:

  • Portal for customer service - This can be a dedicated page where your customers can search for solutions to common questions, which we even call FAQs. Not just that, there can be forums in which your customers can participate and have their vote heard.
  • Portal for your partners - Maintaining collaboration between your partners can be at a time a gruesome task. This bridges the gap between them and results in informed communication, resulting in visual growth of the business.
  • Portal for customers - If a customer is willing to see all of their history related to interaction with the service agent, grievances reported earlier, its solution, and all other details around it, you can create a separate portal for that. Giving your customer ease of access to every detail is a game changer.
  • Completely customized portal - This is where you can put your brand's name on top of all the customizations that you want to do. You can completely revive the experience of your brand, with the help of Experience Cloud. The best part being, everyone will see you and your brand rather than Salesforce being showcased as the driving force.

Easy customizations are available where you can play with the available templates, and with just click and drop, you can create whatever you can think. Integrations are also available so that you can see your data across different channels talking to each other. Customers can see all the progress of their issues and their solutions, while partners can interact with each other to make an informed decision.
