Sales Cloud- A customizable automated sales process


Sales Cloud is an automated sales management tool, which assembles all the scattered data over different devices in one place. Whether you are a salesperson or a sales manager, you have all the information handy on your laptop or even mobile. With the help of Sales Cloud, you get a streamlined sales process, which can be customized and automated at your convenience. Sales Cloud is a Customer Relationship Management (CRM) which is provided as SaaS (Software as a Service) and this service will help you in generating more business which in turn will increase your revenue. Want to learn more about it? KeyKloud, with extensive 10 years of experience in Salesforce, will help you in customizing the service, which impacts your business in a better way.

It is an application built on the Salesforce platform which helps companies to manage their sales process. Starting from the instance where the potential customer first interacted with you to the last day when they buy your product/service and become your customer, it keeps everything at a centralized place. With this, anyone in the sales team can access the information anytime from anywhere.

Features of Sales Cloud:

  • Open for all - Sales Cloud can be used for both business-to-customer(B2C) and business-to-business (B2B) verticals.
  • Office on the go - With the Sales Cloud being browser-based and app-based, you can easily track all the activities on a deal. All the interactions can be recorded on the go, which will simultaneously be synced and reported to your manager. This will keep everyone, involved in the sales process, on the same page.
  • Automate the entire sales process - With the help of workflow creation, easy to follow path can be created, completely customized as per your sales process. This will make everything efficient, including the process of approvals which usually takes more time.
  • Keep a track of all conversations - With Account and Contact objects; you can keep a track of all the activities between your sales team and the customer. Not just that, you can get the new empowering tool of social media integrated right into your Sales Cloud. This will help you in gaining some crucial insights about the customer.
  • Don't lose opportunity - With the Opportunity object, you can view every detail of a deal at the same place. Be it the negotiations, quotes, products/services being offered, their pricing, and everything that revolves around it.
  • Create campaigns and generate leads - How about if you get all the details of all of your marketing campaigns in one place. Yes, that is possible only with Sales Cloud. With the Campaign object, you can create, monitor, and analyze the data collected during the campaign. Related information about the potential customers can then be stored in the Leads object automatically, saving you both time and effort.
  • Monitor growth with graphs - The first page that you will come across when you open the application is the dashboard. This is where you will get a real-time picture of your sales and just with a click you can get a detailed version of the process. All the reports can also be accessed from the dashboard for further analysis.
  • Be prepared for what's coming - With the help of a forecasting tool, you can make an informative decision on your sales process. You can have a look at the future of your sales and make adjustments accordingly to generate more revenue.
  • Be in touch with your customers - Chatter enables you to be in contact with your customers round the clock. Not just that, you can be in contact with your team as well and help them out if they hit a roadblock.
