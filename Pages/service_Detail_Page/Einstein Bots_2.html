Einstein Bots- Your Company's virtual assistant


Bots, a term derived from robots, is a digital way of transforming human interaction. Usually, your agents are busy resolving critical issues and maybe at that moment, a customer is looking for some straightforward answer. These bots sit on your website or your app, pre-configured with FAQs, using which a user can get their answers without waiting for an actual company representative to answer their query.

With the recent developments in the area of artificial intelligence (AI), Salesforce has created a chatbot that works on Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Natural Language Understanding (NLU). This bot is named Einstein Bot; thanks to the intelligence that's driving the bot. Understanding your customer's question and then processing it to give the most appropriate answer is what this bot does. They behave as a helping aid to the agents by collecting some of the initial information from the customer and providing answers accordingly.

With the repeated questions being answered directly, Einstein Bots are intelligent enough to connect your customers with a human when faced with a critical question or situation. This saves time for the customer as soon as they start interacting with the Einstein Bot, by answering their questions. At the same time, if the chat needs to be diverted to an agent, they already have the required information about the customer and the concern, which makes their job easy. KeyKloud helps you in customizing Einstein Bot with the related queries, making it your customers' most intellectual initial contact with your company.

With your business, be it a product-related query or a service issue, certain queries are repeated. Einstein bots are programmed with the answers to those questions, handling various customers at the same time, parallel. This creates a seamless process that lets your agents perform at their maximum potential. Einstein Bot is well integrated with all the database systems in the backend, ensuring that the information is verified and a related solution is provided to the customer in the very first go. This is more of a self-service for the customers as people love to solve their queries on their own, with a bit of support from Einstein Bot.

Why use Einstein Bot:

  • Resolve recurring issues in the very first interaction without waiting for long queues over the call.
  • Self-help is another way of making your customers feel valued and independent.
  • Reduce the time spent during the call holds or long queues, even for a simple answer.
  • It is a one - time setup, helping agents when they are overloaded with complex queries.
  • It understands the human language, and thus, can respond accordingly.

Features of Einstein Bot:

  • Can be integrated on your website or on your mobile app.
  • 24*7 availability of an agent that has answers to most of your customer's queries.
  • AI-enabled bot which can understand the complexity of the issues and can redirect the customer to a customer service representative.
  • Can be easily customized to enhance customer satisfaction while interacting with the bot.
