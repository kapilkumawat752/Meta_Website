Field Service Lightning- A powerful tool for off and on-field agents



Field Service Lightning allows you to excel in the area of field services provided to your customers. Facilitating your customers with on-time, accurate and hassle-free service is what helps you in retaining the customers. This is the key to dominate any vertical of the market. Just imagine, how convenient and successful it can be if your field agents are provided with a solution through which:


  • They can be on time at every specified schedule.
    • Have all the necessary tools and equipment in stock and handy.
    • On the go knowledge base, to help them do things in a specified way, and on the spot customer feedback retrieval.

All this and much more can be achieved by using Field Service Lightning. Easy to configure, automated monitoring of all the field calls, and complete control over the entire process is what this service aims to provide to you. It takes care of all the important factors of your business viz. Customers, Field Agents, In-office Agents, and the entire inventory. Moreover, it gives you a complete view of your field agents as to how much time they are spending on the customer service calls. With all this being based on Salesforce, everything is available on mobile devices, reducing the turnaround time.


Roles of people involved:


Admins – With the use of Core Field Service Lightning, administrators can customize the features as per the business model. Whatever you want to track, you can get it done through your administrators or Metadologie can help you in the setup process.


Agents – This is the very first set of people of your company to whom your customer interacts in the very beginning. They are the ones responsible for noting the customer issues and creating related work orders. Being integrated with various objects like Accounts, Contacts, Cases, and many more, the work order has all the necessary details which a field agent will require to do their job with any roadblocks.


Dispatcher – With the defined skills and area of the field agents, the dispatcher assigns work orders accordingly. They use Field Service Lightning Managed Package which includes dispatcher console, giving you a top-level view of all the activities of the day including all the schedules, tracking status and location of the field agents, inventory check for all the services of the day, and much more.


Field Agents/Technicians – With Field Service Lightning Mobile App, these agents can then manage their appointments. They will have all the job-related information on their mobile device including the contact details, inventory management of the required parts, and a huge glossary of articles related to assigned jobs. 

Benefits of using Field Service Lightning

These are some of the key benefits listed, which are just the preview. The entire package will revamp the field service capabilities of your company.


  • The very first benefit is real-time tracking. This is useful for both in-office and on-fields agents. They can track the progress of the work orders scheduled in the day and can also keep their managers updated with the latest status of the work order.
    • Automatic assignment of the jobs based on technician's working hours, their location, their skill set, and any other business rule based on the SLA.
    • Access to information on mobile devices.
    • All stock is updated before every schedule so that your technician has all the required parts when at the customer's location.
    • Optimize available resources and inventory as per the company policies and schedule.
    • Graphs and charts presenting real-time monitoring of all the activities related to a work order. Be it an installation, or a repairing, or a maintenance service.
    • Chatter feature to interact with the in-office agents for various reasons or even scheduling a next day call.
    • Knowledge articles are readily available for doing even the trickiest jobs on the field, ensuring 100% customers satisfaction every time.
